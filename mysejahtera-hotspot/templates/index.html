Get the code and data at https://github.com/huseinzol05/project-suka-suka/tree/main/mysejahtera-hotspot. Click on any polygons to get average case, total resolution points covered and area covered in km^2.

Yellow color covered 0%-33% percentile of active cases, Orange color covered 33.1%-66% percentile of active cases and Red color covered 66.1%-100% percentile of active cases in each state. Minimum 3 resolution points to assumed 1 cluster. Distance between one point to another point approximately ~0.33km, except for Sabah ~0.55km dan Sarawak ~0.66km.
